Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-29. This policy explains what the Community Complaint Map collects and your rights under the GDPR and CCPA/CPRA.

What we collect

  • Account data — email address and an optional display name.
  • Report content — the description, category, photos/media, and the precise location (latitude/longitude) you attach to a report. Report pins are shown publicly on the map.
  • Technical data — your IP address, stored only for abuse and rate-limiting purposes and purged from reports after 90 days.

Why we use it (lawful basis)

To operate a civic reporting platform: publishing your report to the community, attributing it to your account, scoring report quality, and preventing spam/abuse. Public display of report location is based on the consent you give when submitting.

Who we share it with

  • Supabase — database and media storage.
  • Sentry — error monitoring (configured to exclude personal data).
  • Google — only if you choose "Sign in with Google".

We do not sell personal information (CCPA).

Your rights

  • Access / portability — export everything we hold via GET /api/auth/export/ (or your profile → "Download my data").
  • Erasure — delete your account from your profile. Your stored IP is scrubbed; published reports are retained anonymously as civic records.
  • Correction — edit your reports and display name at any time.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the platform administrator listed in the project README.
